  • How do I schedule a blood test through the WellBee Website/App?

    You can book a health test on the WellBee Website/App through the following ways:
    You can also choose tests based on your Health Risk areas by selecting from various health risks listed on our homepage. You can also choose tests based on your lifestyle and habits.
    For specific tests, search in the "Search Box".
    Once you have selected the package click on the book now option.
    Enter your phone number and the OTP you receive. Update details of members who you want to add on for the test. Update a convenient date and time for sample collection.
    Select a payment method and complete your order.
    Once you complete your order, your summary page will open with details of your booking.

  • How might a health checkup help you feel better?

    Doing a regular body check-up can help doctors diagnose a disease before it poses high risk. Reduces the risk of complication during treatment once you are diagnosed with a health condition at an early stage, the complexity and risk involved are less when compared to diagnosing at a very late stage.

  • What are the locations where we offer a full body checkup?

    WellBee provides full body test in covers all the major cities on Pan India with Free Home Sample Collection.

  • I'm not sure which health package I want?

    You can chat with our health experts via the chat window at bottom right corner of the website & provide details of your health condition to get the right advice on preferred tests.
    You can also call us 9811401180 to get recommendations.

  • Can I add more than one member for a test in a transaction?

    Yes, you can add more than one member for a health test. While placing your order there will be an option for you to select the number of members you want the test for.

  • Are there any specific requirements for taking the tests?

    Fasting maybe required in case of a few tests to ensure accurate results.
    In general it is also advisable to restrict from alcohol and heavy diet at least for 24 hours before any health test. You can see the requirement for each test on the product page.

  • How can I make the payment for my test booking?

    You can pay for your tests through net banking, debit card, credit card, or UPI.
    Once you have made the booking you will see your order and booking summary on the app & website and will also receive an SMS with details of your booking and sample collection slot on your registered mobile number.

  • How do I know my booking is confirmed?

    You will receive an SMS with the details of your booking and your sample collection time slot on your registered mobile number immediately after you book a test.
    You can also check "Cart" section in your app or on your account on web.

  • Which payment method do you accept?

    We have various payment options available.
    You can pay through online banking, debit card, credit card, & UPI.

  • Is it safe to book with my Debit/Credit card?

    Yes, similar to your other transactions via debit or credit card, paying for your tests is completely safe, we have secure payment gateways and platform audits.
